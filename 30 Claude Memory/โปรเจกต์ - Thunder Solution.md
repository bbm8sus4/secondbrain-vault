User is **COO of Thunder Solution Co., Ltd.** (บริษัท ธันเดอร์ โซลูชั่น จำกัด · Khon Kaen, Thailand) — slip verification + LINE chatbot business serving Thai e-commerce / online merchants.

## Two brands under Thunder Group

- **Thunder Solution** — enterprise/large customers (LINE Bot, Thunder API, Flex)
- **EasySlip** — SMB-focused (Easy Bot, EasySlip API)

## Product mix (8 May 2026 snapshot)

- Thunder Bot — biggest customer base (53K) but lowest ARPU ฿120
- Thunder API — VIP customers, ARPU ฿826
- Flex — newer product, growing fast
- Easy Bot — SMB chatbot, ARPU ฿94
- **EasySlip API** — STAR product, ARPU ฿986, 60% of group revenue, MRR ~฿2.5M

## Key metrics & business pattern

- **Annual revenue target:** ฿125M (split: Thunder ~฿55M + EasySlip ~฿70M)
- **Unit economics:** 82% margin per slip (revenue ~19 satang, cost ~3 satang)
- **Volume:** ~16M slips processed in 8 days · ~2M/day average
- **Customer base:** ~67K total users · only 16% active (huge re-engagement opportunity)
- **Pre-paid billing cycle:** Margin always shows -40 to -50% in first week of month, recovers by month-end. **THIS IS NORMAL — do not flag as crisis**
